Process

Three steps. Five minutes.

1

Export

TenantSync packages your entire Dataverse solution into a portable format — every flow, every table, every dependency.

2

Transfer

The package uploads to a secure cloud hub and transfers to the target tenant. No USB drives. No email attachments.

3

Activate

Connections are mapped, the solution imports, and every flow activates. You verify. You're done.
